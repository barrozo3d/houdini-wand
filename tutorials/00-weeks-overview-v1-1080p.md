### Full Content [0:00]
**Transcript:** In the first week you will recognize the power of Udini and what distinguishes the software from other 3D packages. We will find out the differences between Udini versions and we will talk about the hardware requirements. Next we will get into the interface and we will start our first project of the course. So we will be modeling Thoris Hammer, which we will grip it later to shoot at Iron Man a great storm of race. At the end of this first week you will be able to move around the interface, understand the procedural workflow, model a high quality asset, create shitters and make render to submit your asset with a professional final look. On the second make we will set up the scene to start working on the final project and we will animate Thoris Hammer that we modeled previously using chops in Udini. Once the scene is ready to work we will get on with the main race setup, ray ramifications and extra rays. After completing the second week you will have learned how to control some of the most important nodes of Udini as well as how to use VOP to modify geometry and create ramps for a better control of attributes. On week 3 we will talk about points, particles and collisions. We will introduce you to these three concepts quickly and we will develop a simple example making use of two systems to get to know the advantages, disadvantages and differences. Then we will continue working on the final project and we will set up the geometry to create a spark system. Here you will acquire the necessary knowledge to develop and control particle systems in an efficient way. Week 4 will be a week dedicated to volumes. Here we will learn everything about volumes, what they are and how they work. We will work on a particular example while learning all the necessary concepts and tools to control the powerful pyro solver and understand how volumes work. We will then continue creating the smoke pass caused by the raise impact we have developed in week 2. By the end of this fourth week we will have already created all the effects of this first season project. Week 5 will be all about bringing everything together to create the final result. Although this is not a shading and compositing course, we will teach you how to create materials for the different effects of this course, make render passes and compose the shot using cops in Udini. At the end of this course you will be able to compose your own version of the shot and submit your project with a professional final look.
